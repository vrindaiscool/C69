Luna Park!
I visited Luna Park on the 8th of October!

Luna Park is a very funplace to visit as it has a variety of fun and enjoyable rides. AS well as this it has many yummy and tasty food shops within the area. On my birthday they also gave me a special badge and card that allowed me to have 3 free rides!How cool is that! Overall Luna Park is an amazing fun place to visit as it has a wide variety of rides and food, not forgetting the amzing way they greet you on your birthday!
